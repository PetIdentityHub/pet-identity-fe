Core Concepts

PetProfile (PetIdentity)

PetProfile serves as a digital manifestation of our pets' lives, much like our own digital footprints. As humans, we have traces of our education, work experience, places we've been, medical records, and personal connections. These all contribute to our identity and how others perceive us. Pets are no different in this regard.

Creating a PetProfile is the first step towards consolidating all of a pet's experiences in one unique, ID document-like location. You provide essential information about your pet, then collect digital "Pieces" or footprints, such as veterinary visits, breeder certificates, and other pet-related activities. These Pieces are then linked to your pet's profile.

You have the freedom to use these Pieces as you wish - share them with others or keep them private for you and your pet. PetProfile offers a centralized and convenient way to store, manage, and access your pet's information and history.

Issuer

Every identity requires another entity to verify our experiences, much like a birth certificate confirms our place and date of birth, or a university diploma validates our knowledge and expertise in a specific field. Our pets, too, have such entities. Veterinarians maintain health records, contest organizers provide proof of participation, and animal breeders authenticate pedigrees.

Becoming an issuer not only streamlines the process of issuing and verifying proofs but also contributes to a more sustainable and eco-friendly approach. Being an issuer can help build the reputation of the institution, demonstrating their commitment to transparency, security, and innovation.

Operator

The Operator is a crucial entity within our project, designed to ensure the authenticity and credibility of issuers. Operators possess sufficient privileges to confirm the identity of individuals or institutions applying to become issuers. They serve as a gatekeeper, maintaining the integrity and trustworthiness of our platform.

Piece

Piece is the core component of our solution, serving as the "digital fingerprint" that represents various aspects of a pet's life. Leveraging the power of Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) or Soulbound Tokens (SBTs), Pieces can be registered and minted by issuers, and then collected and associated with a unique PetProfile.

By collecting and connecting these Pieces to a PetProfile, pet owners can create a comprehensive, digital portfolio of their pet's life experiences and accomplishments, which can be shared or kept private as desired.
