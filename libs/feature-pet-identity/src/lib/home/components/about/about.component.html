Why Blockchain?

PetIdentity harnesses blockchain to secure pet data, ensuring tamper-proof e-identities. This decentralized approach fosters trust, enables seamless ownership transfers, and promotes transparency in pet management.

How it works?

PetIdentity generates unique Soulbound Tokens (SBTs) for pets, storing vital info on the blockchain. This ensures secure access, traceable ownership, and easy data sharing among pet owners, vets, and authorities.
